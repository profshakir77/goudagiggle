# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing and commerce pages in `artifacts/gouda-giggles`
- Homepage (`/`)
- Shop and category views (`/shop`)
- Product detail pages (`/product/:id`)
- Gallery (`/gallery`)
- Quote / lead-generation page (`/quote`)

## Out of scope
- Cart and checkout flows (`/cart`, `/order`, `/order-confirmation/:id`)
- API routes under `/api/**`

- Local customers in Latham, Albany, Schenectady, Troy, and the Capital Region looking for charcuterie boards, grazing tables, and event catering.
